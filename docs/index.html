A Tool to digitally manage
your company everyday workflows

Yester is a tool that helps you to create and manage your everyday processes. It is like a lego, where you can create workflows unique to your company or choose one from our library.

Examples of
Workflows

Here are some examples of processes Yester can help improve. Note that many of them are not considered processes at all, but simply “part of working life”. Whatever the name, Yester makes them more efficient and faster.

Personnel

  • Vacation leave applications Create a vacation application followed by approval based on company structure. There is access to vacation balances, previous vacation applications and current state of ongoing applications. Everyone can see the company's holiday schedule and receive information about the upcoming holiday or other activities related to their holiday. Managers can create holidays for several people at once, and to simplify the work, it is also possible to confirm holiday applications by e-mail. For automatic data flow it is possible to integrate Yester with various payroll software, incl. Merit Palk, Taavi Palk and Pilvepalk.
  • Sick leave notification Notify your team that you are sick. Everyone will see from the calendar that you are not working, and notifications go directly to your manager. When you are feeling well, you can end your sick leave.
  • Business trip application It is possible to make an application to go on a business trip, to issue expenses or simply to inform colleagues that you are away from the office. In the company calendar it is possible to see who and at what period is away from the office.
  • Home Office notification To avoid unnecessary searching for people in the office, it is possible to inform colleagues about working in your home office. It is up to you to decide whether the notification is simply by adding absence to the calendar or whether the team will receive, for example, an email.
  • Training applications To go to the desired training, make an application and ask your manager or, for example, the human resources manager. The company's calendar will reflect your absence.
  • OnBoarding When your new colleagues are joining the team, you wish they would feel welcome and valued from day one. With Yester you can make onboarding more simple, automated and have overview of the entire process. It is possible to order all the equipment they need, rights to different systems, buildings or set up trainings. It all happens through workflow that can already be set before so that all equipment or access rights needed from IT go to people responsible from IT department, training request to person responsible for certain training etc.
  • OffBoarding Offboarding is a process that has received less attention than it deserves. With Yester you can customize your offboarding so that process is seamless, and nothing is left without attention. Make sure that the final discussion has taken place, all equipment has been returned, user rights have been removed etc.
  • Requests for work equipment Order equipment for yourself or for your team (new computer, shoes, work clothes etc). The task is received by the responsible person or department. Then the employee can confirm they have received the equipment. There is an overview of all received equipment therefore you will have an overview of your company’s assets.
  • User rights Give and withdraw user rights whether it is software or an access to a premise. The request goes to the correct person or department who can make needed changes. You will have a list of all granted and withdrawn rights.
  • Personnel data change Whether your bank account number or something else have changed, you can notify it through Yester and make sure it will not be lost in all the e-mails.

Everyday tasks

  • Customer suggestions and complaint management Customer feedback can come in different forms. Gather it through suggestions or complaints, they are very important source of information. Yester helps you manage the process unique to your company involving everyone that need to be part of it by creating a suitable workflow and providing an overview of the current state of a customer suggestions or complaints.
  • IT ticketing system Let your IT know of suggestions or make a ticket to solve a problem. Users know who is dealing with their issue and when a solution could be found.
  • Fleet logbooks Create logbooks of your company vehicles milage or compensate your personnel for using their own transportation. With Yester you can keep track all business trips made by company or personal vehicles.
  • Purchase orders Manage your purchases by creating purchase orders and set workflows and rules for its approvals.

Production

  • Safety audit Make sure your working environment is safe and create automated safety audit workflow that suit your company and standards.
  • Systems / Equipment Administration and Maintenance Register Keep track of all your equipment and their maintenance by issuing responsible persons and automated notifications.
  • Manage your process from system or equipment failure to repair Manage your process from system or equipment failure to repair. Process Report a device failure and get a response from the management team on how long it may take to repair.

We aim to create value

Yester is flexible and can be used to accommodate very different and unique workflows. Forget process management with pen, Excel or outdated tools starting from vacation management, non-conformities, equipment management, employee onboarding or offboarding. Yester will give you clear overview of process statuses.

Doing same thing several times by different people waste time and is real work motivation killer. Yester helps you set your processes in a clear path so every task has a responsible person or a group. Yester integrates your current software to reduce excess manual work.

In Yester you set the order of tasks to be done and have clear understanding who is responsible of current task. Everyone have understanding of who has the ball and what needs to be done to move forward.

Processes will be faster as the path has been set and everyone knows what to do and when. No wondering who is responsible now or is it already done.

Receiving client feedback requires immediate attention. In Yester everyone who needs to be involved will be involved through notifications with overview of the matter in hand. No chain e-mails, duplicated problem solving or forgetting to react.

Yester allows you to react to your current needs by simply developing your workflow. For example, if purchase manager needs to be involved in a client feedback process it is quick and seamless. Or your company decides to reduce approvals of a vacation process - it’s just a quick change and can be done without weeks of development.

With Yester your everyday workflows will be seamless and “Yes it’s done” will be common frase.

How do
we
do it?

Everything that can be described can be yestered.

Start with the workflow you wish to improve. Think it through so it would be simple and without “waste”.

What information needs to flow in the process? Data could come from users who input it through forms or from other software. Also add all notifications that need to be delivered to uses in different workflow stages.

Think of the users who would use the workflow, what are their user roles and approval route. The route could come from company hierarchy or you could create unique route using groups.

What would be the languages users would be most comfortable with? In Yester you can translate processes to different languages.

It is usual that process do not start or end in Yester. Think of the integrations needed and let’s make it happen.
